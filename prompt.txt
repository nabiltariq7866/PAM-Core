AI-Powered Lender-Controlled Appraisal & Valuation Intelligence Platform
For PAM Group / PAM Value
🎯 Demo Objective

Demonstrate a next-generation appraisal operating platform that helps PAM Group strengthen its position as a lender-controlled alternative to traditional AMC workflows.

The demo should show how PAM can help lenders:

· Manage appraisal orders internally without relying on expensive traditional AMC markups
· Maintain Appraiser Independence Requirements through automated rotation, separation controls, and audit trails
· Build, manage, and monitor their own approved appraiser panels
· Reduce borrower appraisal costs while improving transparency, turnaround time, and quality control
· Automate appraisal order intake, appraiser matching, revision handling, compliance review, and final delivery
· Give lenders, appraisal desks, processors, compliance teams, and leadership real-time visibility across every appraisal order
· Use AI to predict turn-time risk, coverage gaps, appraiser capacity issues, revision likelihood, and valuation risk
· Integrate with LOS platforms like Encompass and other lender systems while keeping PAM as the intelligence layer on top

PAM already has a strong market angle: appraiser-owned, lender-controlled, lower-cost, transparent, and compliance-aware. The demo should make that positioning more technical and more defensible by showing a real execution platform, not just an order portal.
